About Ma'rifa 2022

Reviving Hearts

“Neither My earth nor My heaven can contain Me. I am contained only in the heart of My believer.”

We are so excited to host Ma’rifa 2022: Heart & Revival, our third annual conference for Shi’i youth in Washington, D.C. on March 25th-27th. Once again in person, the conference will bring together ulema, academics, community organizers, and hundreds of Shi’i students to engage with a range of topics, strengthen their faith, and learn to be more effective leaders. We will adhere to strict COVID-19 guidelines and will provide virtual options for those who cannot be with us in person.

Join us this March to revive the commitment in our hearts to become believers who can truly know and love God and His creation.

COVID-19

In an effort to maintain the safety and health of all conference attendees and personnel, we have set in place COVID-19 safety guidelines that will be enforced. Everyone at the conference venue is expected to follow these protocols and guidelines.

Proof of Vaccination (including booster)

Masks in all indoor spaces

Outdoor eating only

What is Ma'rifa

Ma’rifa is a national organization committed to the spiritual and intellectual growth of Shi’i students. Our goal is to invest in and enrich the Shi’i student journey in order to discover our individual and collective Ma’rifa.
